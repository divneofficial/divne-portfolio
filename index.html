Diane Cortez (born September 15, 1999) better known by her stage name DIVNE, is a Filipina emerging singer-songwriter from Toronto, Canada.

Growing up with older sisters and a brother, she was influenced by many genres such as 90s r&b and alternative rock, while singing along to hit songs on a karaoke machine. Being named after soulful Diana Ross and Princess Diana, she knew she was meant to rock the stage while being charismatic and elegant. Her divine RnB voice catches the listener’s hearts with catchy pop hooks which describe the vulnerability and emotions she has felt in her life that many youths can relate to.

She is currently working on her debut as a new artist and building a community on her Twitch channel, where she performed in many online "Open Mic Nights". Whilst working on expanding her audience, she is also working on releasing her first five singles.
